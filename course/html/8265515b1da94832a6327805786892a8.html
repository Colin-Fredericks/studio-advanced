Links for Gnurds

You stuck around until beyond the end of the course! Hardcore, my friend.

For those still looking for more places to go with their explorations of edX, we present a small number of curated links. These are for the intrepid, the daring... and also for those who test before they deploy.

  • The Stanford Demo Course, which will show you a lot from their Open edX instance.
  • The venerable and antique Open edX Data Documentation, which never moved beyond Version 0.1. Some of it still works, some of it doesn't.
  • MIT's latex2edX scripts, which let you build an entire course completely in \(\LaTeX\). Includes open-source demo course.
